Sonalika DI 50 Sikander is widely used for agricultural and related uses by farmers. It is one of the leading tractors in terms of popularity and sales in the utility tractor range. Sonalika DI 50 Sikander tractor is preferred by farmers who have an average agricultural area and use farming implements like cultivator, trailer, rotavator, and seed drill regularly.



This tractor is equipped with modern elements and a powerful engine delivering a total output of 52 HP, Sonalika DI 50 Sikander is one of the popular choices among farmers. To deliver this performance, this engine is connected with a 10-speed gearbox having 2 reverse and 8 forward gears. Features

Sonalika DI 50 Sikander has 8 forward and 2 reverse gears delivering the maximum top speed of 34.4 Kmph in forward gears. With a 6 spline PTO setup, the tractor delivers a PTO HP of 44 HP at 540 RPM. Additionally, the tractor is known for its excellent performance in off-road and on-road conditions. This is due to the 6 x 16 Inches and 14.9 X 28 inches tyre setup in front and rear respectively. The lifting performance is improved due to the ADDC hydraulic controls used in the system. Also, Sonalika DI 50 Sikander delivers long productive hours on farms due to its fuel-efficient engine and a fuel tank capacity of 55 litres.


Special features

Sonalika DI 50 Sikander is capable of delivering a maximum torque of 205 mm. This tractor is available with mechanical and power steering options.
