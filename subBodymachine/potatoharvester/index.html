Potato Harvester features:

   Potato harvesters are machines that harvest potatoes. They work by lifting the potatoes from the bed using a share. Soil and crop are transferred onto a series of webs where the loose soil is sieved out.

   The potato digger elevator consists of a shovel, rod chain conveyor, gearbox and two gauge wheels. The machine digs one or two ridges of potatoes at a time and picks up the soil-potato mass over the rod chain conveyor. Agitating sprocket, of this conveyor oscillates the rod chain and separates the soil.
