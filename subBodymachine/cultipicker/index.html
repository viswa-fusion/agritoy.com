Tractor Cultipacker

   Our range tractor cultipacker (HC- 606) is widely appreciated for its high operation efficiency, rugged construction, low maintenance and no noise and air pollution. Our flip-over cultipacker efficiently crushes dirt clods, presses down small stones and removes air pockets. It thus helps in the preparation of a smooth and firm seedbed where seeds is sown. The cultipacker roller gently firms the soil around the seeds to ensure shallow seed placement and excellent seed-to-soil contact.
